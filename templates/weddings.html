Our Wedding Process

1

Complimentary Consultation

Share your vision with us during a personalized consultation. We'll discuss your wedding theme, color palette, favorite flowers, and budget to ensure we create the perfect floral experience.

2

Custom Proposal

Based on our consultation, we'll create a detailed proposal with mood boards, flower selections, and pricing tailored to your unique wedding vision.

3

Refinement

We'll work together to refine your selections until everything is exactly as you've dreamed. Adjustments can be made up to three weeks before your wedding.

4

Flawless Execution

On your wedding day, our team handles all setup and installation, ensuring every detail is perfect so you can focus on enjoying your special day.
