Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I receive my flowers?

You can choose between weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly deliveries. You can change your delivery frequency at any time through your account settings.

Can I skip a delivery if I'm away?

Absolutely! You can pause your subscription or skip a delivery at any time. Just log in to your account and manage your delivery schedule.

What if I'm not home for the delivery?

Our delivery partners will leave your flowers in a safe place at your delivery address. You can also add delivery instructions to your account for special handling.

Can I customize the flowers in my arrangement?

Our subscriptions feature seasonal selections chosen by our designers. However, you can note preferences in your account (like color palettes or flowers to avoid) and we'll do our best to accommodate them.

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, our flowers typically last 7-10 days. Every delivery includes care instructions to help you maximize the lifespan of your arrangement.
